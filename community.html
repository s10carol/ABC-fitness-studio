Giving Back to Our Community

We are committed to making a positive impact far beyond our studio walls.

🎗️ Charity Fitness Events

Every quarter, ABC Fitness Studio partners with local charities to host fundraising fitness events. 100% of proceeds go directly to the selected organization.

🏫 Local School Partnership Programs

We partner with local Dallas schools to bring free fitness education to students. Our certified instructors host after-school fitness workshops to help young people build healthy habits.

🌍 Free Community Fitness Days

On the first Saturday of every month, ABC Fitness Studio opens its doors to the public for completely free group fitness classes. No membership required.
